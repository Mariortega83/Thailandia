  • Day 1

    Arrive in Bangkok and Flight to Krabi

    Route: Arrive in Bangkok, then take a flight to Krabi.

    Accommodation: Krabi Town

    Description: Arrive in Krabi in the morning. After a short rest, spend the afternoon exploring nearby beaches like Ao Nang. In the evening, walk around the local night market in Krabi Town and try some delicious Thai food.

  • Day 2

    Railay Beach and Thung Teao Forest Natural Park

    Route: Take a boat trip to Railay Beach.

    Accommodation: Railay Beach

    Description: Spend the day at Railay Beach, famous for its towering limestone cliffs and crystal-clear waters. In the afternoon, visit the Thung Teao Forest Natural Park and explore the Blue Pool, a stunning turquoise natural pool.

  • Day 3

    Phi Phi Islands

    Route: Take a boat to Phi Phi Islands.

    Accommodation: Phi Phi Don

    Description: Explore the Phi Phi Islands, visit Maya Bay, and enjoy snorkeling and relaxation on the beautiful beaches. In the evening, dine at a beachside restaurant.

  • Day 4

    Koh Lanta

    Route: Take a ferry to Koh Lanta.

    Accommodation: Koh Lanta

    Description: Spend the day exploring Koh Lanta's peaceful beaches like Klong Dao and Long Beach. Rent a scooter to visit the Koh Lanta National Park and enjoy a sunset on the beach.

  • Day 5

    Koh Lanta and Surroundings

    Accommodation: Koh Lanta

  • Day 6

    Return to Bangkok

    Route: Take a ferry from Koh Lanta to Krabi, then fly to Bangkok.

    Accommodation: Bangkok

    Description: After breakfast, take the ferry to Krabi and a flight back to Bangkok. You can spend some time shopping or sightseeing before heading to your international flight.
